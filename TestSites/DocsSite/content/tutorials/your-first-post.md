## Goal

Create and publish your first blog post using Oneka.

## Steps

1. Open your Hugo site in Oneka.
2. Navigate to the `posts` section.
3. Click **New Post**.
4. Fill in the title and body.
5. Set `draft: false`.
6. Save.

Your post will appear in the posts list immediately.
