Designed and shipped a patient-facing mobile app for Riverstone Health on iOS and Android.

## Challenge

Patients needed a way to view lab results, schedule appointments, and message their care team from a single app.

## Outcome

Launched to 12,000 active patients in November 2025 with a 4.7-star average rating in both app stores.
