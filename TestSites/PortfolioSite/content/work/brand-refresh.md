A full brand identity refresh for Apex Corp, including logo redesign, typography system, and website relaunch.

## Challenge

Apex's visual identity had not been updated since 2012. The brand felt dated and did not reflect the company's current positioning.

## Outcome

Delivered a new logo, color system, and component library that rolled out across web, print, and product in Q4 2025.
