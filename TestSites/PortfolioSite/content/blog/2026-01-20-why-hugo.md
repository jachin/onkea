After years of managing WordPress sites for clients, we made the switch to Hugo for all new static marketing sites. Here is why.

## Build speed

Hugo builds a 500-page site in under two seconds. That matters when clients are iterating on content.

## Security

No database, no PHP, no login page to brute-force. The attack surface is the CDN and nothing else.

## Content ownership

All content lives in Markdown files in a git repo. Clients own their content outright and can move hosts without a migration project.
